Music Collection Completer

Designed for people have large collections of albums, this is a Windows app that analyses your music collection and tells you which albums you are missing.

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

Get started

Download version 1.0 here and unzip to any folder on your computer. Then just run MusicCollectionCompleter.exe

Note: Windows support only. See all releases.
